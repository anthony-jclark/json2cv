Software Contributions
2014-

Developer: Developer: Evolve-a-Robot: Online Evolutionary Robotics Environment

URL: http://evolve-a-robot.github.io/

Code: https://github.com/evolve-a-robot/evolve-a-robot.github.io

Description: The web portal allows users to conduct their own evolutionary robotics experiment while changing parameters related to evolutionary operators and the environment. Users develop an understanding of how population size, mutation, crossover, and selection influence the evolution of a wheeled robotic system. The portal can be accessed directly by individuals, or in the context of a workshop to facilitate group discussion and individual student exploration.
